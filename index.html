The process

How CivicLens works

Data Aggregation

We ingest official data from government databases, contract registries, and public records‚Äînormalizing it into a consistent, searchable format.

Blockchain Anchoring

Critical data and documents are hashed, and those hashes are permanently recorded on a public blockchain for tamper-proof verification.

Public Access

Everyone gets a clear, searchable view of projects with real-time updates, evidence uploads, and an open API for researchers and journalists.

Why it matters

Transparency drives accountability

Every year, billions of rupees flow into public infrastructure. Yet delays, cost overruns, and opacity remain endemic. CivicLens changes that by making every project, payment, and milestone visible‚Äîcreating a permanent, verifiable record that empowers citizens and strengthens governance.

  • Reduces corruption and misuse of funds
  • Enables data-driven journalism and advocacy
  • Builds public trust in government institutions
