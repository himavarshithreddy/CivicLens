How it works

  1. Aggregate

    Ingest and normalize official data on projects, contracts, milestones, and payments.

  2. Anchor

    Compute content hashes and anchor them on-chain for a permanent audit trail.

  3. Verify

    Provide a clear public view with evidence, alerts, and an open API.
